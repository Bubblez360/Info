# PRODUCT.md — Angelo "Deps" Depamaylo Portfolio

## What this is

Personal portfolio for Angelo Depamaylo (Bubblez360) — BSIT graduate (ZPPSU 2026),
full-stack developer and IT specialist from Zamboanga City, Philippines. The site is
his primary online presence for the job market.

## Register

**brand** — design IS the product. A visitor's impression is the deliverable.

## Audience

1. HR managers and technical recruiters (primary — decide in seconds)
2. Clients and startup founders (freelance work)
3. Developers (judge craft: code, motion, details)

## The one job

A visitor must leave knowing: who he is, what he builds (TindaPOS first), what
he works with, and how to reach him — and think "this person ships real things."

## Truth constraints (hard rules)

- Only repo-backed or resume-backed claims. Nothing invented.
- Certs: Cisco Networking Basics (Apr 2024), Claude 101 (Jun 2026), Claude Code 101 (Jun 2026).
- "BSIT Graduate, ZPPSU 2026." No LinkedIn link (unconfirmed). No phone number.
- No fake screenshots — project cards are text-first until real assets exist.
- All copy lives in src/data/content.ts (single typed source, schema-validated at build).
